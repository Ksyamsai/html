ABOUT:

Allu Arjun (born 8 April 1983) is an Indian actor who primarily works in Telugu cinema. Known for his dancing abilities, he is a recipient five Filmfare Awards South and three Nandi Awards. One of the highest paid actors in India, he has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2014. After his debut in Gangotri (2003), Allu appeared in Sukumar-directed Arya(2004) .
